Shipping Policy

Processing Time

Orders ship within 3-5 business days. During high-demand drops, processing may take up to 7 business days. You will receive email confirmation when your order ships.

Shipping Options

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free on orders over ₹2,000)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (₹200)
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day (₹500)

International Shipping

We ship to select countries worldwide. International shipping takes 7-14 business days. Customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the customer.

Tracking

All orders include tracking information. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Track your package through our shipping partners' websites.

Delivery Issues

If your package is lost or damaged during shipping, contact us immediately at hello@tragy.com. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue.

Contact

Shipping questions? Email us at hello@tragy.com
